THE EFIMER

IDEA


Efimer is a project based on the concept that fashion is ephemeral. To represent this idea i designed a haute couture collection made by vegetables, wich are ephemertal too. i decided to paint the vegetables to give uniformity at the collection and express the purity that provides the white color.
